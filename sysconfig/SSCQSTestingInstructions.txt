Once you have read these instructions click on "continue" to start the testing. A screen will be presented.  Click on "Next >>" to show an image.  Then click on the rating scale at the bottom of the screen and move the slider to an appropriate quality rating. Click on "Rate" after you have decided a rating. Click on "Next>>" after you have rated an image to display the next image. The count displays the number of images seen by you and the total number of images in the set.
